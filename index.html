About Me

I am an Urban and Regional Planning graduate with over 2.5 years of professional experience in GIS, Remote Sensing, and spatial analysis. My expertise lies in land-use analysis, climate & environmental monitoring, and developing data-driven insights for planning and decision-making.

My research focuses on applying machine learning with remote sensing data to assess meteorological drought and groundwater vulnerability in Bangladesh.

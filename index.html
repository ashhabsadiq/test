Research & Publications

Meteorological Drought Assessment in Northern Bangladesh

Journal: Ecological Indicators (Q1) | 2023

This study applied a Random Forest (RF) model, integrating the Standardized Precipitation Index (SPI) with seven remote sensing indices to evaluate drought intensity from 2010 to 2019. The analysis produced a set of drought distribution map, demonstrating the effectiveness of combining geospatial data and machine learning-based modeling for environmental monitoring.

GIS Remote Sensing Machine Learning GEE Random Forest Drought Mapping SPI Python R

Advancing Groundwater Vulnerability Assessment in Bangladesh

Journal: Groundwater for Sustainable Development (Q1) | 2024

This research delved into the complex dynamics of groundwater vulnerability. By investigating topographical, meteorological, socioeconomic, and land use & geological factors through a machine learning model, this research provided a comprehensive analysis, shedding light on the various aspects contributing to groundwater vulnerability of Bangladesh.

Groundwater GIS Machine Learning Remote Sensing Python GEE Categorical Analysis

Students' Perception on Environmental Sustainability in Higher Education Institutions

Journal: Frontiers in Engineering and Built Environment (Q2) | 2023

This investigation assessed students’ perceptions of campus environmental sustainability (ES) across three universities in Khulna, Bangladesh. Using survey-based analysis, it evaluated awareness, attitudes, and willingness to participate, providing insights to support sustainability-focused policymaking and campus planning.

Environmental Sustainability Universities GIS Questionnaire Survey SPSS Statistical Analysis

Community-Based Approaches for Sustainable Outcomes: A case study

Conference: International Conference on Advances in Civil Engineering (ICACE-2024)

This study examined the application of participatory rural appraisal (PRA) methods in a rural community, focusing on community engagement, local knowledge, and decision-making processes. The findings highlight how participatory approaches can support environmentally informed planning and community-level sustainability initiatives.

PRA GIS Sustainable Planning Survey Community Engagement Focused Group Discussions
